# 📚 Probability & Statistics Study Hub

This workspace is set up as a project memory space and tracker for studying the open-access textbook **[Introduction to Probability, Statistics, and Random Processes](https://www.probabilitycourse.com/)** by **Hossein Pishro-Nik**.

> [!NOTE]
> This textbook is intended for undergraduate as well as first-year graduate level courses. It covers basic probability, statistics, limit theorems, random processes, and simulation.

## 🗺️ Recommended Study Paths

Depending on your learning goals, you can follow one of these paths:

### 1. Core Probability & Statistics Path (Standard)
*Focuses on building a solid foundation in probability theory and mathematical statistics.*
* **Chapters:** 1 ➔ 2 ➔ 3 ➔ 4 ➔ 5 ➔ 6 ➔ 7 ➔ 8 ➔ 9

### 2. Stochastic & Random Processes Path
*Ideal for engineering, signal processing, and systems analysis.*
* **Chapters:** 1 ➔ 2 ➔ 3 ➔ 4 ➔ 5 ➔ 6 ➔ 7 ➔ 10 ➔ 11 ➔ 15

### 3. Computational & Simulation Path
*Ideal for data scientists and programmers who want to learn simulation alongside theory.*
* **Chapters:** 1 ➔ 2 ➔ 3 ➔ 4 ➔ 5 ➔ 6 ➔ 7 ➔ 12 (MATLAB) ➔ 13 (R) ➔ 14 (Python)
